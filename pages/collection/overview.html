Yes, our curly hair extensions are made from 100% human hair that has been carefully selected to ensure that the cuticles are intact. This means that the hair is less likely to frizz and will match your natural hair texture more seamlessly. Remy hair is also less likely to tangle than other types of human hair extensions, making it a great choice for kinky, coarse, or curly hair. Our Remy hair extensions are available in a variety of lengths and colors, so you can find the perfect match for your unique style.

Our curly texture is purely natural with no processing or perming whatsoever, this natural curl will last over time just like your real hair. Our kinky curly and relaxed straight textures go through a non-chemical steam perming routine. This involves wrapping the hair around metal rods and blasting it with steam to acheive the curl pattern. The curl pattern may loosen over time after washing and using heat styling tools.

If you're considering curly hair extensions, you need to be prepared to put in some extra effort to keep them looking their best. Curly hair tends to tangle and matte easily, so it's important to use the right products to keep it hydrated and healthy. Look for sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners that will help to retain moisture and prevent tangles. In addition, consider using a leave-in conditioner or serum to help keep your real human hair and your hair extensions looking shiny and vibrant. With the proper care, your curly hair extensions will look beautiful for months to come.
